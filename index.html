About UNGADRAKEN
Photographer
Editorial / Fashion / Personal Work

Raw texture.
Emotional surface.

Visual storytelling focused on raw texture, atmosphere and emotional intensity. Working across editorial, fashion and personal projects with an interest in youth, intimacy, tension, cold light, bodies in space and the emotional residue that remains after the image has been taken.

Editorial instinct Magazine structure Noise and scratches Body as symbol Provocation through form

Approach

The work moves between fashion image, diary fragment and visual fiction. The goal is not polish for its own sake, but atmosphere with pressure: something tactile, charged and slightly unstable, like a printed page that has been handled too many times.

Interest

Texture, skin, fabric, gesture, suburban melancholy, nightlife residue, awkward closeness, staged realism and the point where editorial beauty starts to feel slightly dangerous.
