About UNGADRAKEN

AN ARCHIVE OF IMAGES

UNGADRAKEN is a photographic archive built around editorial image-making, atmosphere, surface, clothing, gesture and contemporary visual culture. The site is structured less like a portfolio and more like a sequence of stored material: pages, fragments, entries and selected images gathered into one continuous archive.
Editorial Fashion Personal work Archive format

Approach

The images are presented with as little interruption as possible. Scale, repetition and spacing are used to keep the feeling of turning pages rather than scrolling through thumbnails.

Format

The structure draws from catalogues, printed matter, archives and artist books: restrained, direct and built to let the work hold the surface.
