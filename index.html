About UNGADRAKEN
Photographer
Editorial / Fashion / Personal Work

Raw texture.
Contemporary image.

Visual storytelling focused on texture, atmosphere and intensity. Working across editorial, fashion and personal projects with an interest in the visual language of the present: surface, tension, styling, stillness, constructed moments and the emotional charge that remains around an image.

Editorial instinct Magazine structure Noise and scratches Image culture Surface and form

Approach

The work moves between fashion image, visual fragment and staged document. The aim is not polish for its own sake, but atmosphere with pressure: something tactile, clear and slightly unstable, like a printed page that keeps carrying traces of handling, repetition and time.

Focus

Texture, fabric, gesture, contemporary styling, emotional distance, nightlife residue, image saturation, closeness, form and the point where an editorial image begins to feel charged beyond its surface.
